Dogs become valuable employees at bank

It was revealed this morning that a major high street bank has begun employing clever canines to perform basic data-entry tasks in several of its UK data centers. In a statement earlier today a spokesperson for the bank said that the training had gone well with thefirst intake of ten pooches and that overall they are happy to be involved.
